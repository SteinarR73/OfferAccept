\# OfferAccept — Product North Star



\## Core Definition



OfferAccept is a \*\*deal closing platform\*\*.



It enables teams to:



\- send proposals, quotes and onboarding documents

\- collect verified acceptance

\- generate tamper-evident certificates

\- prove acceptance later through public verification



The core workflow:



Create deal  

→ attach documents  

→ send for acceptance  

→ recipient accepts  

→ certificate generated  

→ certificate can be publicly verified



\## Primary Value



OfferAccept solves one specific problem:



\*\*Closing deals with verifiable acceptance.\*\*



It replaces fragile workflows such as:



\- email approval

\- unsigned PDF proposals

\- unclear acceptance history



\## Target Users



Primary ICP:



\- agencies

\- consultants

\- freelancers

\- SMB service companies

\- software companies selling B2B services



These teams frequently send:



\- proposals

\- quotes

\- offer documents

\- onboarding agreements



\## Product Promise



OfferAccept provides:



\- fast deal sending

\- verified acceptance

\- proof of agreement

\- audit trail



\## Non-Goals



OfferAccept is intentionally \*\*not\*\*:



\- a full CRM

\- a project management system

\- an accounting tool

\- a complex document editor
