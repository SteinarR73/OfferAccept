# OfferAccept — Product Positioning

## Category

OfferAccept sits between:

CRM → Proposal → Acceptance → Onboarding

OfferAccept focuses on the **acceptance and proof layer**.

## Short Description

OfferAccept helps teams send deals and collect verified acceptance.

## Competitive Position

OfferAccept does not compete directly with DocuSign.

DocuSign is a general e-signature platform.

OfferAccept focuses on **deal acceptance workflows**.

## Primary Differentiators

1. Verified acceptance certificates
2. Public certificate verification
3. Delivery timeline visibility
4. Lightweight deal workflow

## Messaging

OfferAccept should be described as:

**The simplest way to close deals online.**

or

**Send deals. Get verified acceptance.**
