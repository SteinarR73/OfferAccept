\# OfferAccept Product Roadmap



\## Phase 1 — Core Product Hardening

Completed



\- Inline document upload wizard

\- Public certificate verification

\- Sender notifications



\## Phase 2 — Activation Improvements



Next priorities:



\- First deal onboarding

\- Empty state UX

\- Activity feed on dashboard



Goal:

Ensure new users send their first deal within minutes.



\## Phase 3 — Workflow Expansion



Planned features:



\- Deal templates

\- Reminder system

\- Quote blocks



Goal:

Increase reuse and reduce friction when sending deals.



\## Phase 4 — Platform Growth



Future capabilities:



\- multi-recipient deals

\- stronger identity verification

\- integrations with CRM systems
