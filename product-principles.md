\# OfferAccept Product Principles



1\. Simplicity first

Sending a deal must always feel fast and simple.



2\. Verified trust

Acceptance must always generate proof.



3\. Clear workflows

Users must understand what happens next.



4\. Visible outcomes

Accepted deals should feel like important moments.



5\. Focus over feature count

A smaller number of excellent workflows is better than many incomplete ones.
